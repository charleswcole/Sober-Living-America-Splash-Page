About Us

Who Are We?

Sober Living America is a community service organization that has served over 50,000 families suffering from addiction over the past 20 years.

What do we do?

Through providing affordable sober living housing,food, recovery and spiritual education, as well as a work development program- we have created a model that drastically changes the lives and futures of those we serve.

Where does the money go?

100% of the proceeds from our charity event services serve the Sober Living community.
